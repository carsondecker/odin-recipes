Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is one of the simplest and quickest meals to make for yourself.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • American cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread and generously butter one side of each.
  2. Place a slice of cheese between the two slices of bread, with the buttered sides on the outside.
  3. Place the sandwich on a hot skillet until brown on one side, then flip and do the same on the other side.
