Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Spaghetti aglio e olio is a simple and quick dish that anyone can make.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and salt it heavily.
  2. Add the spaghetti to the boiling water and cook until just before al dente.
  3. Bring a pan up to medium heat, add 8 cloves of carlic, then season the oil with red pepper flakes.
  4. Put the pasta into the oil along with some pasta water, then coat it in the sauce.
  5. Add some parmesan and chopped parsley to top of the pasta.
