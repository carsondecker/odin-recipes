Smash Burger

Description

Smash burgers are a quick and delicious meal to make for a family.

Ingredients

  • 80/20 ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Canola oil
  • American cheese
  • Hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Divide ground beef into loose balls and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat until very hot and add canola oil.
  3. Put 2 balls into the pan at a time and smash them with a spatula as much as possible.
  4. Cook until brown on one side, flip, and put a slice of cheese on top.
  5. Once the other side of the patty is brown, place the patties on a bun with whatever toppings you enjoy.
